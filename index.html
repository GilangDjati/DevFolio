Featured Projects

Four case studies that blend marketing insights with frontend craftsmanship—each built to validate a hypothesis and report measurable lift.

Disaster Data Management Application

Challenge: Regional responders needed a secure way to sync victim records in real time. Solution: Built a React + Express platform with role-based access, incident tagging, and map overlays. Result: Cut manual spreadsheet work by 70% and provided live visibility for local teams.

  • Stack: React, Redux Toolkit, Express.js, SQLite, Tailwind CSS, SweetAlert2.

Air Quality Monitoring System

Challenge: Communities lacked real-time guidance during forest fires. Solution: Delivered a responsive dashboard that pulls OpenWeatherMap data, flags risk levels, and suggests health actions. Result: Enabled local officials to broadcast advisories within minutes of AQI shifts.

  • Stack: React, Redux Toolkit, Tailwind CSS, OpenWeatherMap API.
Live Demo GitHub

Digital Literacy Awareness – PKM-PM

Challenge: Elementary students needed guidance on responsible gadget use. Solution: Produced interactive workshops, coding games, and take-home toolkits with a reporting microsite. Result: 95% of participants completed the digital pledge and teachers integrated the material into weekly lessons.

  • Stack: Workshop Facilitation, Content Strategy, Code.org Games, Documentation.
Live Demo GitHub

SME E-Commerce Mobile App

Challenge: Local merchants were tracking sales manually. Solution: Created an Android app with inventory, order, and customer modules plus analytics exports. Result: Reduced reconciliation time by half and gave owners visibility into repeat purchase trends.

  • Stack: Java, SQLite, Android SDK, Material Components.
